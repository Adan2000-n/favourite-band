members of the band

E-sir

The legendary E-Sir was a Kenyan hip hop artist. He was born Issah Mmari Wangui on May 20th, 1981 and died on the 16th of 2003. He was the elder brother to fellow Kenyan rapper Habib. He was famous for his lyrical ability and command of the Swahili language as well as Sheng.

kaligraph jones

Khaligraph Jones (born Brian Ouko Robert, 12 June 1990) is a Kenyan hip hop artiste. Jones started in the Kenyan entertainment scene way in 2009 after he won the Kenyan Edition of the Channel O Emcee Africa competitions at the age of 19.
